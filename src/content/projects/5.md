Framewell helps people quickly lay out photo walls without guessing measurements or proportions.

- Drag-and-drop planner for arranging frames on a virtual wall
- Built-in templates for common layouts and room sizes
- Shareable links so clients and collaborators can review and tweak the design
- Automatic sizing guides that translate your digital plan into a ready-to-hang setup

It turns the usual tape-and-paper hassle of gallery wall planning into a fast, collaborative, and confidence-boosting workflow.
